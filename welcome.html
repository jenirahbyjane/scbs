Welcome to the Sports Equipment Reservation System for Mara Professional College, Beranang!

At KPM Beranang, we understand the importance of staying active and maintaining a healthy lifestyle. To facilitate your journey towards a more active and exciting campus life, we are pleased to introduce our Sports Equipment Booking System. Whether you're an avid athlete or just want to have fun with friends, our platform is designed to make sports gear more accessible to you.

How does it work?
Our Sports Equipment Ordering System is a user-friendly, efficient and easy way to order sports equipment for your various athletic needs. Here's how it works:

1. Browse Our Catalog: Explore our extensive catalog of sports equipment, including basketballs, soccer balls, tennis rackets and more. We offer a wide range of options to suit a variety of sports and activities.

2. Book Your Gear: Once you've found the gear you need, simply select the item, select your preferred pick-up date and place the order.

3. Pick up and Enjoy: Go to our designated pick up location on the selected date. Submit your booking confirmation, and our friendly curriculum staff will provide you with the equipment.

4. Have fun: Now, you're ready to have fun on the field or court. Play your favorite sports, stay active and enjoy your time on campus.

Keys Features:
Easy Booking: Our intuitive online platform makes it easy to browse and book sports equipment with just a few clicks.

Wide Selection: We offer a complete range of sports equipment, ensuring you have access to the equipment you need for a wide range of activities.

Flexible Pickup: Choose a pickup date that fits your schedule, making it convenient for you.

Quality Assurance: All our equipment is well maintained and regularly inspected for quality and safety.


Get started!
Ready to start your sports adventure at KPM Beranang? It's as easy as 1, 2, 3:

Log in to your college account on our platform.
Browse our equipment catalog and place your order.
Pick up your gear, play, and have fun!
We are excited to be a part of your journey to a healthier and more active college life. If you have any questions or need help, our friendly team is here to help. Visit us at the Student Development Center, Co-Curriculum Room.

Let's make your time at KPM Beranang more interesting and satisfying through the excitement of sports. Keep playing, and have a great time!
